Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test (DMIT)

The Scientific Map to Your Inherent Potentials

What is DMIT?

Decoding the unique biological blueprint of your brain through the science of fingerprints.

DMIT (Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test) is a scientific assessment based on the study of skin ridges on fingers and palms. These patterns are directly linked to the lobes of the human brain, providing a clear map of an individual's innate intelligence, talent, and personality traits.

Unlike traditional IQ tests which can be influenced by environment and mood, DMIT measures your Inborn Potentialâ€”the blueprint you were born with. This 100% safe and non-invasive test helps in identifying how a person's brain is naturally wired to process information.

"Fingerprints are the external representation of the internal brain."

The Science Behind DMIT

A multi-disciplinary approach combining biology, psychology, and technology.

Genetics

Fingerprints are formed by highly concentrated DNA coding and remain unchanged throughout a person's life.

Embryology

Brain and fingerprints develop simultaneously in the womb between the 13th and 19th week of pregnancy.

Neuroscience

Each finger corresponds to a specific brain lobe (Frontal, Parietal, Temporal, Occipital) and its neural pathways.

Dermatoglyphics

The statistical study of dermal patterns which has been used for over 200 years in medical and psychological research.

What DMIT Identifies

Our comprehensive analysis provides data on over 100 metrics of your personality.

  • Multiple Intelligences: Linguistic, Logical, Musical, Spatial, etc.
  • Quotient Mastery: Precise scores for IQ, EQ, CQ (Creativity), and AQ (Adversity).
  • Brain Dominance: Understanding Left-Brain (Analytic) vs Right-Brain (Creative).
  • Learning Styles: Visual, Auditory, or Kinesthetic learning preference.
  • TRC Count: Reflecting the total learning capacity of the brain.
